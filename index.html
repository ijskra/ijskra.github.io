About Me

OH Jung-Woong (aka Jake) is a composer, performer, and producer. At Indiana University in Bloomington, he has been studying music composition with P.Q. Phan and Eugene O'Brien, harpsichord with Elisabeth Wright and Jonathan Oddie, and computer music with John Gibson and Chi Wang. His musical interest and passion are solely on human emotion, the entity fully unutterable with human language. His musical exploration and experiment are only to expand the scopes and boundaries of "logical space" of human emotion. For that, he tries with any means he can. His musical interest and production encompass diverse styles from Renaissance Madrigal to microtonal guitar quintet with live-electronics, from Jazz Standard piano trio to metal band, from dark techno to chiptune-styled K-Pop. He often plays keyboard himself as a performer and he has remarkable knowledge and swift intuition for improvisation of any genres and basso continuo. His music has been performed and broadcasted numerous times throughout Asia, North America and Europe.
